RISK ANALYSIS UPDATE
- Moved production year selector beside the Risk Analysis title.
- Added total guarantee amount loaded by selected production year.
- Removed all requested explanatory texts.
- D/E = due debt / total guarantee amount.
- Baht/ton = (due debt + additional promotion) / contract tons.
- Revenue = contract tons × total cane price including CCS.
- Expense = contract tons × cutting/loading/transport cost.
- Income difference = revenue - expense.
- Net profit = income difference - due debt - additional promotion.
- Kept Save entered values and Reset defaults buttons.
- Responsive on landscape, portrait, tablet and phone.
